About Us

Welcome to VEYL VENUE, where a blend of luxury and warm hospitality awaits you. Established in 2023, our hotel is a haven of comfort and sophistication located Chennai. With a commitment to delivering exceptional experiences, we offer a range of meticulously designed rooms and suites to cater to every guest's unique preferences. Indulge in culinary delights crafted by our skilled chefs, explore our state-of-the-art meeting and event spaces, and enjoy personalized service from our dedicated team. Committed to sustainability, we strive to minimize our environmental impact, ensuring a guilt-free stay. Conveniently situated near Guindy, Veyl Venue is not just a place to stay; it's your gateway to the best the area has to offer. Come, let us redefine your idea of hotel living. Book your stay today and experience the perfect blend of comfort and sophistication at Veyl Venue.
